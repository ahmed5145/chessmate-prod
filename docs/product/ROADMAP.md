# Phased Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP Batch Coach

Goal: ship a believable coaching product quickly.

- Manual multi-select batch analysis.
- Shortcut to choose the most recent N games.
- Single combined report.
- Coach-style summary.
- Top weakness detection.
- Basic progress status.
- Clear action plan.

Exit criteria:

Users can pick games, run a batch, and get a report they understand without explanation.

## Phase 2: Better Coaching Depth

Goal: make the report more diagnostic and more personal.

- More precise recurring-pattern detection.
- Better opening diagnosis across the batch.
- Improvement focus by phase and theme.
- Better comparison between current batch and prior batch.
- Exportable summary or shareable report.

Exit criteria:

The product can tell users what changed and what to work on next.

## Phase 3: Long-Running Analysis Experience

Goal: support larger batches without blocking the user.

- Let users close the tab during long jobs.
- Email the report when analysis is done.
- Reopen saved reports from history.
- Larger batch sizes and slower but deeper analysis when needed.

Exit criteria:

Users can start a heavy job and safely return later without losing the result.

## Phase 4: Coaching System

Goal: turn the product into an ongoing improvement loop.

- Training plans tied to recurring weaknesses.
- Batch-to-batch progress tracking.
- Personalized drills or study recommendations.
- Subscription or credit package refinement based on usage.

Exit criteria:

The app behaves like a recurring coach, not a one-off analyzer.

## Deferred Ideas

- Fine-grained billing optimization.
- Human coach integrations.
- Rich visual dashboards that do not change decisions.
- Full opening repertoire management.
